Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. Similar technologies (local storage, pixels, SDKs) have the same effect and are treated here as "cookies" for simplicity.

2. Why we use them

Yolki Pet uses cookies to:

  • Make the site work correctly (authentication, preferences);
  • Remember user choices (language, light/dark mode);
  • Understand how the product is used, to improve it (analytics, consent-only);
  • Ensure security (fraud and abuse prevention).

3. Categories we use

CategoryPurposeLegal basisTypical retention
EssentialLogin, session, security, theme/language preferenceLegitimate interest / contract performanceSession up to 12 months
AnalyticalAggregated usage and performance measurementConsentUp to 14 months
FunctionalReminders between visits (partial form, list position)Legitimate interest / consentUp to 12 months

We don't use behavioral advertising cookies or share data with ad networks.

4. Manage cookies

You can:

  • Adjust your preferences in the preferences panel on the site;
  • Block or delete cookies through your browser settings (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge);
  • Enable private/anonymous browsing mode.

Note: blocking essential cookies may prevent some features from working properly.

5. Consent

On your first visit, we display a banner to collect (or decline) consent for non-essential cookies. You can change your choice at any time through the preferences panel, with no need to provide a reason.

6. Sub-processors

Any analytics are performed by contracted providers with data protection clauses aligned to GDPR and LGPD. The updated list is available upon request at hello@yolki.pet.

7. Changes

We may update this Policy to reflect technical, legal, or product changes. The current version is always the one published on this page.

8. Contact

Questions about cookies or privacy: hello@yolki.pet
